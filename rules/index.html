Setup

  1. Place your ships either horizontally or vertically (not diagonally) on open squares. Do this in secret!
  2. You must put your ships on the grid. Not off the grid. Not overlapping with other ships.
  3. After you decide your ship placement and the game begins, ships can't be moved.
  4. No peeking at your opponents ship layout. This is considered cheating.

Rules

  1. Flip a coin to decide the order in which turns are taken.
  2. On your turn, pick a target square and call the letter and number out that it corresponds to.
  3. On your opponents turn, listen to what target square they call out and if you have a ship placed over that square, mark it with a red X.
  4. Continue until all the ships of one of the players is destroyed. The last player with surviving ships wins!
